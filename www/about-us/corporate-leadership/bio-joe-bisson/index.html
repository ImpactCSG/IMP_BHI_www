Joe Bisson, Vice President, Business Development

As Vice President, Business Development for BHI, Joe Bisson drives the company’s mission of providing self-driven data assets to participating Blue companies and leveraging those assets with other Blue companies and external organizations to improve healthcare costs. Drawing on his extensive experience in the healthcare informatics industry, Bisson develops new analytical products for Blue companies and external partners and further expands the strategy for continued BHI growth, including sales force development.

Bisson brings more than 20 years of expertise to his role with the company, including experience in sales management positions with industry leading organizations HBOC/McKesson, Solucient, Health Market Science and ActiveHealth Management. He has worked with Blue Cross and Blue Shield companies throughout his career, including, NASCO, Blue Cross Blue Shield Association and Consortium Health Plans (CHP).

Bisson holds a Master of Business Administration with a focus in marketing from the University of Maryland, a Master of Science in statistics from the University of Miami and a Bachelor of Science in statistics from the University of Florida.
