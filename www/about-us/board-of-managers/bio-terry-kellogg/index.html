Terry Kellogg, F.S.A., President and CEO, Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Alabama

Terry Kellogg is President and Chief Executive Officer of Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Alabama. He joined Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Alabama in 1982 as Vice President & Chief Actuary. He has also served as the Company’s Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, Executive Vice President and President and Chief Operating Officer.

Mr. Kellogg is the current Chairman of the Interplan Programs Committee of the Blue Cross and Blue Shield Association. He also serves as the Board Chairman for the United Way of Central Alabama and is a member of the American Academy of Actuaries and a Fellow of the Society of Actuaries.
