    BHI Website Privacy Policy

    Your privacy is important to us. Health Intelligence Company, LLC, d/b/a as Blue Health Intelligence maintains high standards for the protection of your privacy at our websites. Here is what you can expect when you visit bluehealthintelligence.com: 

    The Information We Collect
    Our web servers automatically capture your domain name, the IP address of the web page from which you enter our site, the pages you visit on our site and the amount of time you spend here. We do not collect any information that can reveal your personal identity unless you voluntarily provide it, for example when you when you complete the login registration. We do not knowingly collect or use information from children under 13 without the consent of a parent or guardian.

    Use of the Information this Site Gathers/Tracks
    Any personally-identifiable information you give us will be used only to provide you with access to bluehealthintelligence.com, to provide the product, service or information you have requested or for which you registered.

    Other than making the information you voluntarily provided upon log-in available in bluehealthintelligence.com your personally-identifiable information will not be shared with third parties, including Blue Plans, and BCBSA without your express consent. Nor will any personally-identifiable information ever be sold, rented, or licensed to third parties without your express consent.

    Making Changes to the Information You Provide to Us
    At any time, you can change, update or remove from our system any information you have voluntarily provided.

    Cookies
    When you visit our site, we may place a cookie on your computer that will allow us to customize and enhance your experience on bluehealthintelligence.com, make improvements to our site, or to report site activity. Our cookies will never be used to track your activity on any third party websites or to send spam, nor will our cookies provide us with any personally identifiable information about you.

    Sites We Link To
    If you choose to use any of the links we provide to our member companies, sponsors, and other third party resources, you will be leaving our website and going to a new website. Protection of your privacy at those other sites will be governed by the privacy policy at that site. Please take the time to read the privacy policies at their sites.

    Changes to this Policy
    From time to time, we may post changes to this policy. You should review this policy frequently.
