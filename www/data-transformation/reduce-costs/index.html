The new reality: comparison-shopping for healthcare.

Just as BHI makes it possible to compare safety and performance among healthcare providers and medical facilities, we’re also able to identify those providers achieving positive results most efficiently.

Exemplifying the power of these new possibilities, BHI analytics facilitates tools such as the Blue Cross and Blue Shield National Cost Comparison Tool for consumers.

The advantages are undeniable for companies as well as consumers and even healthcare providers themselves, particularly in the current, ever-evolving healthcare environment.

Today’s consumers expect more as they become, out of necessity, savvier about the cost variances between one provider and another for the same procedures.

Today’s providers appreciate knowing where they stand and gaining recognition for delivering cost-effective, evidence-based, high-quality care.

BHI analyzes a vast and growing database that currently covers the healthcare experience of 165 million lives, revolutionizing the way companies and consumers shop for healthcare, and how competitive health care benefits are designed.
