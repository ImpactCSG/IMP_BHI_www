Data integration keeps people healthier and saves money.

Powerful BHI data analytics is behind some of the most innovative programs in the healthcare industry, not only improving the bottom line of healthcare costs for employers and their employees, but also helping to save lives.

Using our predictive risk analyses, we can identify people who are at risk for costly, debilitating illnesses in time for intervention and one-on-one support that can help patients reverse their risk or minimize their potential for serious complications.

As a perfect case in point, BHI’s Avoidable Diabetic Hospitalization Model makes early intervention possible with patients who are at risk for a hospitalization due to diabetes. Using this information, healthcare companies and providers can directly provide patients targeted health management strategies, either in the office in real time and or ongoing via other communication and educational support mechanisms (other support mechanisms include health-risk assessments, interactive on-line tools, 24-hour nurse support telephone lines and wellness programs, among others).

The result: Better healthcare, improved outcomes and lower costs.
